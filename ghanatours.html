Kakum National
Park

Kakum is the first national park in Ghana created by local initiative as opposed to that created by the State Agency responsible for Wildlife. Fifty-two villages and an agricultural landscape consisting primarily of food crops and cocoa surround the park.

Mole National
Park

In 1958 Mole Game Reserve was established, enclosing some traditional hunting grounds and sacred sites. In 1964 all the inhabitants of five villages in the southern part of the park were resettled elsewhere.
